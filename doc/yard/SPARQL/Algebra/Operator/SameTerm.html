Overview

The SPARQL sameTerm operator.

Examples:

(prefix ((xsd: <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#>)
         (: <http://example.org/things#>))
  (project (?x ?v)
    (filter (sameTerm ?v)
      (bgp (triple ?x :p ?v)))))
